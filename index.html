The following is a list of easily playable games in the Sonic/Metroid franchise in chronological order. Older Browsers can go here: Classic It is for use as a marathon list:

    Rules:

    • You must watch each movie all the way through.
    • No skipping or fast forwarding.
    • Each movie can be watched at any stage in a day.
    • Watch one movie a day
    • No skips or major glitches.
    • Total gameplay per day must be a max of 1 hour.
    • Save state of simply save at the end of the hour if you can.
    • Can be played at any time of day.
    • Games must be played and completed in the order below.
    • NO DEBUG MODE OR CHEATING DEVICE (I.E ACTION REPLAY) ALLOWED!
    • Cutscenes cannot be skipped.
    • The person who beats the final game (Metroid: Other M) first, wins!
    • Metroid Prime Trilogy can be played in place of Metroid Prime 1,2,3.
    • Games in Green are not yet released.
    • Games in Blue can be skipped.
    • Level Select allowed where needed, you may go back to the act you Game Overed on. (ie. Sonic 1 Mega Drive, Springyard Zone Act2)
    • No skipping acts/zones
    • Must beat game to the final ending. (i.e Must beat all characters story in Sonic Adventure / Sonic 06 etc.)
    • Games can be, but are not required to be, beaten 100%.
    • Total gameplay per day must be one full 1 hour.
    • Save state at the end of the hour if you can.
    • Can be played at any time of day.
    • Extra sonic games can be added ( i.e Other Ports/Fangames, but must be agreed on and placed in the order which you put them in.)
    • Games must be played and completed in the order below.
    • NO DEBUG MODE OR CHEATING DEVICE (I.E ACTION REPLAY) ALLOWED!
    • Have Fun, even on the bad games.
    • The person who beats the final game (Sonic Forces) first, wins!
    • Re-Releases or other ports can be used, as long as they do not differ substantally to the original/other ports or have a speed advantage.
    • Loading time advantages are okay (i.e Xbox360 vs. PS3)
    • They can not be played if featured on the list below unless it is a bonus game and is going to skipped.
    • Optional games (in blue) are okayed to be skipped.
    • ROM Hacks (in Red) are okayed to be skipped. (ROMHacks.zip)
    • Games in green are not yet released.
    • You can skip red games and not blue games and vice versa.
    • Optionally Sonic 3 Complete can be played instead of Sonic 3 and Knuckles (More info: https://info.sonicretro.org/Sonic_3_Complete. I personally recommend it.)
    • Also check out the official website,; for some customisation options not avaliable in the ROM's options menu.
    • You must complete Sonic 3 and Knuckles with both routes.(Sonic/Tails and Knuckles)
    • For the S1 RLG set the level length to extreme (13km) and play one level or any length levels that add up to 13km.
    • Oh, by the way, you must play the Sonic Heroes tutorial without skipping any dialogue.
